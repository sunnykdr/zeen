How it works

A clean process that keeps everything simple: brief, concepts, refinement, and final delivery.

Clear milestones

01 — Brief & Direction

We collect your requirements, references, and brand vibe. You’ll receive a clear direction before we start designing.

02 — Concepts

We share initial logo/identity concepts with explanations. You choose one direction to refine.

03 — Refinement

We improve balance, spacing, and usability. Revisions happen here until the final design feels premium.

04 — Final Files

You receive print + social exports (PNG/JPG/PDF/SVG as applicable), plus basic usage guidance.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

What do you need from me to start?

Your business name, what you do, preferred style, colour likes/dislikes, and 2–3 reference logos you admire.

Do you deliver editable files?

Yes. For Standard/Premium packages you can receive vector formats (PDF/SVG) depending on the package.

How many revisions are included?

It depends on the package. Basic includes 2 revisions; Standard includes 4 revisions; Premium includes more refinement as needed.

Can you do urgent delivery?

Yes, urgent delivery is possible depending on availability. Share your deadline and we’ll confirm.
